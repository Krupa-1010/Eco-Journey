Steps To Take Before Travel

Pack Light

Minimize your luggage to reduce carbon emissions and travel footprint.

Plan Your Trip Mindfully

Research and choose destinations that prioritize sustainability and conservation.

Choose Eco-Friendly Transport

Opt for trains, buses, or carpooling to minimize your travel impact.

Carry Reusable Products

Bring reusable water bottles, bags, and utensils to reduce waste.

Green Travel Tips

Choose Carbon Offset Option On Flights

Flights account for 2.5% of global carbon dioxide production. Opt for carbon offsets when booking flights.

Stay In Eco Hotels And Home Stays

Select accommodations that conserve resources and support local ecosystems.

Support Local Communities

Buy from local artisans and vendors to support the local economy and reduce carbon emissions.

Respect Wildlife and Nature

Keep a safe distance from wildlife and follow guidelines to protect natural habitats.

Limit Use of Plastics

Refuse single-use plastics and dispose of waste responsibly.
